Welcome to DICL

DANIEL INSTITUTE OF CHRISTIAN LEADERSHIP

DICL was born out of a passionate call that identified gaps in leadership, especially in the body of Christ.

Founder, “Rev. Daniel Muraya”

For years, Rev. Muraya trained leaders but noted that consistent training is key to equipping them. DICL now offers leadership training and mentorship at both junior and senior levels.
